## How it works

1. **Request** — Employee or team lead describes the AI use case.  
2. **Negotiate** — Autonomous agents (Legal, IT, Procurement, Works Council) agree parameters.  
3. **Enforce** — Deterministic gateway applies PII rules, routing, and budgets.  
4. **Prove** — Audit trail ready for DPO and regulators.
